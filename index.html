About Us

A boutique Portugal specialist, based in Porto.

North Senses Tours was built around a simple idea: Portugal deserves more than a bus schedule. We're a small, licensed operator based in Porto, working exclusively in private tours and transfers — which means every itinerary is planned around the traveler in front of us, not a fixed departure time.

Most of our guests come from the United States, drawn to a country that still feels unhurried, authentic and — for many — quietly familiar. We take that seriously: every guide and driver speaks fluent English and knows how to bridge the two cultures, from wine terminology to what "eating late" actually means here.
